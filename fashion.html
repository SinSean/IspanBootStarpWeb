Apparel business

We aim to make street fashion something that can be enjoyed more freely.

Our vision of street fashion is not just for the younger generation. We offer street fashion that

transcends age and gender, with a mix of sports and modern styles, allowing everyone to freely express their individuality.

X-girl

The brand was launched in the summer of 1994 by Kim Gordon of Sonic Youth and her friend Daisy Von Furth, a former shop staff member at XLARGE.
Drawing on various elements from the street scene, including music, culture, and sports, the brand has adopted the theme of "REAL GIRL'S CLOTHING" and continues to offer "real girl's street style" by offering basic, timeless items that adapt to the changing times.

 

MILKFED.

Sofia Coppola launched the brand in 1995 with her childhood friend, Stephanie Hyman. As a friend of X-girl's founder, Sonic Youth, Coppola was involved with X-girl, including participating in a surprise X-girl fashion show in New York, and was so influenced by the brand that she decided to launch her own.
While inheriting Sofia's concepts of "elegance" and "casual," the brand incorporates trendy elements, attracting many fans. For women who always strive to be "cute," the brand offers a free-spirited fashion style that defies categorization.

 

SILAS

This London-based streetwear brand was founded in 1998 by Russell Waterman and Sofia Maria Prantera, who were both involved in Holmes, the original brand of SLAM CITY SKATES, in East London. The brand's name comes from the fictional character Silas Holmes, who had a major influence on the London street scene. Based on the philosophy that "clothing should be styled in a unique way that reflects the wearer's lifestyle," the brand's collections are characterized by a street-scene base that incorporates British tradition and a high-quality atmosphere. Based on the concepts of "East London street" and "street chic," the brand offers streetwear with a refined atmosphere.

 

ROSE BUD

ROSE BUD was founded in 1993, a time when the term "select shop" was still unfamiliar, as a pioneering women's select shop along Meiji Dori in Shibuya, the mecca of Tokyo fashion. While incorporating the trends of the time, the shop uses its own unique filter to select a wide variety of clothing and accessories from around the world.
"ROSE BUD" means rose bud in English. The brand name embodies the desire to carefully nurture the connections made with people through ROSE BUD, just as a carefully nurtured bud blossoms into fruit.

 

Styles

It started as an exclusive shop when adidas Originals was revived in 2002. It transitioned to a select store format in 2010.
It offers a wide selection of sneakers and offers authentic high street styles that mix street with sporty and luxury styles.
