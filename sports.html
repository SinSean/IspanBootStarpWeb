Sports Business

Sports have always been a source of excitement for people. We support a wide range of sports, including urban sports that have developed as part of street culture, women's sports, and parasports, as well as athletes and people who enjoy sports. We also run skateboarding and dance schools where children can learn while having fun.

ATHLETE

We support athletes who are active worldwide in a variety of sports, including urban sports such as BMX, skateboarding, and breaking. We contribute to creating an environment where athletes with diverse personalities can demonstrate their abilities and charm.

WE LEAGUE

X-girl has been a silver partner and official supplier of the WE League, Japan's first professional women's soccer league, since its opening in September 2021. We will co-create from the perspective of fashion and lifestyle the WE League's philosophy of "contributing to the realization and development of a society filled with diversity in dreams and lifestyles, where each individual can shine, through women's soccer and sports.

X-girl SPORTS

In September 2024, we launched the sports line "X-girl SPORTS" for all women who love sports. It features functionality and silhouettes that support the performance of professional athletes. It's a new generation of sportswear that combines functionality and fashion for women who enjoy both fashion and sports in their own way.
